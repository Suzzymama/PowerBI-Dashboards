# Customer Segmentation & Behavioral Analysis (Power BI)

## 📊 Overview
This dashboard presents insights derived from customer data collected over a 10-year period (2015–2025). It focuses on customer demographics, behavior, and trends to support data-driven decision-making.

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Age group distribution of customers, highlighting the dominant segment  
- Demographic distribution of customers  
- Gender distribution of customers  
- Professional profile of customers  
- Average customer income and age  
- Total number of customers over time  

---

## 📈 Trends and Analysis

### 1. Customer Growth
The line chart illustrates customer growth from 2015 to 2025.

- Rapid growth observed in the first two years  
- Peak reached in **2017**, with customer base increasing more than threefold  
- Significant decline in **2018**, slight recovery in **2019**, followed by another drop in **2020**  
- Stable trend with minor fluctuations from **2021 to 2024**  
- Sharp decline recorded in **2025**

**Insight:**  
Overall, the data shows a downward trend after 2017, indicating potential challenges in customer retention or acquisition.

---

### 2. Regional Distribution
- Majority of customers are located in the **Southern region**  
- The **Western region** has the lowest customer representation  

---

### 3. Age Group Distribution
Customers are categorized as:
- Youth (<25 years)  
- Young Adults (25–40 years)  
- Adults (41–60 years)  
- Seniors (>60 years)  

**Insight:**  
Adults (41–60 years) form the largest segment, while youths (<25 years) are the least represented.

---

### 4. Occupation Distribution
- Engineers represent the largest share of customers  
- Traders account for the smallest group  

---

### 5. Customer Segmentation
Customer segments include:
- Retail clients  
- Corporate clients  
- Private clients  

**Insight:**  
Retail clients dominate the customer base, while private clients have the lowest representation.

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Target **younger customers (<25 years)** through tailored marketing campaigns to increase engagement  
- Investigate the **decline in customer growth after 2017**, especially the sharp drop in 2025  
- Conduct surveys in **underperforming regions (North and West)** to better understand customer needs  
- Develop strategies to attract **underrepresented segments**, particularly youths and private clients
